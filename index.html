♻️ Separate Collection Information

This battery must be collected separately from other waste streams at end of life. It is subject to extended producer responsibility (EPR) under EU Battery Regulation (EU) 2023/1542.

  • Do not dispose with household waste
  • Return to authorized collection point or dealer
  • Battery contains lithium — handle with care
  • Producer take-back program available at manufacturer facilities

🔧 End-of-Life Instructions

Follow these guidelines for safe handling and recycling of this battery at end of life:

  • Discharge to safe voltage level before transport
  • Transport per ADR/IMDG dangerous goods regulations
  • Only qualified professionals should disassemble
  • Recycling efficiency target: ≥ 70% by weight (2026 EU target)
  • Second-life assessment recommended before recycling
